Exam Information

For this Intro to Web Computing (IT 130) there will be a final exam. This exam is a required part of this course and students who neglect to take the exam within the testing window will not receive a passing grade in this course. With this exam, ALL students who are taking this course in any online format are required to take their final exam IN PRESON. Online students should register for an open testing time slot and location as soon as registration becomes available (around 2 weeks before finals week). The exam must be taken in person, and there WILL NOT be any opportunity to complete the Final exam online. There are two ways to take your in person exam. The exam will be facilitated at DePaul on campus for those who are able to come to campus. Because not everyone is able, for those who are can't to come to campus (living more that 30 miles away), the exam may be taken at a licensed testing location.

Another important thing to note is that all assignments in this course have a listed due date. That being said, IF you are unable to complete and turn in an assignment by the listed due date, you still have the opportunity to submit the assignment for partial credit up to two days after the listed due date is the end date. Late work will have 3 points deducted from the per day late.
